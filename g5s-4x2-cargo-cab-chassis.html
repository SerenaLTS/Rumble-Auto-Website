SITRAK G5S 4x2 Cargo Cab & Chassis

Versatile Medium-Duty Performance for Urban & Regional Transport.

The SITRAK G5S 4x2 Cargo Cab & Chassis is designed for flexible freight applications across urban and regional Australia. Combining efficient power delivery, durable chassis construction, and practical driver comfort, the G5S 4x2 is ideal for light-to-medium duty transport operations.

Built for body customisation and fleet integration, the G5S provides a strong foundation for tray bodies, pantec, refrigerated bodies and service vehicle conversions — delivering reliability, fuel efficiency and reduced operating costs.

Overview

The SITRAK G5S 4x2 Cargo Cab & Chassis delivers a cost-effective and adaptable platform for daily freight operations. Designed for flexibility across metro and regional transport, it combines efficient performance, durable chassis construction and practical driver comfort — making it an ideal foundation for light-to-medium duty commercial fleets.

Applications

  • Urban & Metro Distribution — Perfect for city freight, parcel delivery and FMCG distribution.
  • Regional Transport — Efficient and reliable for short to mid-distance regional routes.
  • Body-Build Flexibility — Suitable for pantec, refrigerated body, tray, service body and customised configurations.
  • Trade & Fleet Operations — Ideal for logistics providers, rental fleets and commercial service operators.
