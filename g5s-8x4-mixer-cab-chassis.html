SITRAK G5S 8x4 Mixer Cab & Chassis

Purpose-Built for Concrete & Heavy Construction Operations.

The SITRAK G5S 8x4 Mixer Cab & Chassis is specifically engineered for concrete transport and heavy-duty construction applications. With a high-capacity 8x4 axle configuration, reinforced frame design and Allison automatic transmission, the G5S Mixer delivers stable load distribution, reliable torque output and smooth drivability for demanding site environments.

Designed to support concrete mixer body integration, this configuration offers the strength, balance and durability required for Australian infrastructure and commercial construction projects.

Overview

Built to handle the demanding requirements of ready-mix concrete transport, the G5S 8x4 Mixer Cab & Chassis combines structural strength, balanced axle loading and automatic drivability. Its reinforced mixer-ready chassis and heavy-duty 8x4 configuration provide the stability and durability required for repetitive load cycles and site-based operations.

Applications

  • Concrete Mixer Operations — Optimised for ready-mix concrete transport.
  • Civil & Infrastructure Projects — Suitable for high-demand construction supply tasks.
  • Urban & Regional Building Sites — Designed for stable manoeuvring in tight site conditions.
  • Fleet Concrete Transport — Built for high-frequency commercial fleet use.

Key Highlights

7.36L High-Torque Engine

  • Engine Model: MC07H.35-61
  • Displacement: 7.36L
  • Max Power: 254 kW @ 2200 rpm
  • Max Torque: 1400 Nm @ 1200–1600 rpm

Delivers consistent torque output required for fully loaded mixer operation.

Allison 4430P Automatic Transmission

  • Smooth automatic shifting
  • Improved drivability in stop-start site environments
  • Reduced driver fatigue

Enhances operational efficiency during repetitive loading and unloading cycles.

Heavy-Duty 8x4 Configuration

  • GVM: 31,000 kg
  • Front Axle Design Load: 13,000 kg (total)
  • Rear Axle Design Load: 18,000 kg (total)
  • Main Drive Ratio: 5.29
  • Differential Locks: Interwheel & Interaxle

Ensures balanced weight distribution and improved traction on construction surfaces.

Reinforced Mixer-Ready Frame

  • Trapezoidal ladder-type chassis
  • Main Beam Cross Section: 280 × 80 × 8 mm
  • Wide front (940 mm) / narrow rear (850 mm) frame

Engineered to support mixer body mounting and high-load durability.

Suspension & Stability

  • Front: Parabolic leaf springs (3/3)
  • Rear: Air suspension (2+2+2 configuration)

Provides enhanced load balance and improved ride control.

Advanced Braking & Safety Systems

  • Dual Circuit Compressed Air Brake
  • EBS + ESC + HSA
  • AEBS + LDW
  • Spring Energy Air-Cut Parking Brake

Supports modern safety compliance for fleet and infrastructure projects.
