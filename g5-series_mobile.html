Series Overview

G5 is the adaptable work range for body builders and daily fleet operation.

The G5 Series gives operators practical axle layouts across cargo, mixer and tipper applications. It is suited to businesses that need a strong cab chassis platform, predictable service support and enough configuration choice to match different working routes.

Best matched to

  • Urban and regional cargo body builds.
  • Construction, civil works and quarry support.
  • Concrete mixer and tipper body integration.
  • Commercial fleets needing simple model comparison.
