SITRAK G7S 6x4 Prime Mover

Premium Long-Haul Performance for Modern Fleet Operations.

The SITRAK G7S 6x4 Prime Mover is engineered for high-efficiency interstate transport and heavy combination applications across Australia. Featuring a 12.42L high-output engine paired with a ZF TraXon automated transmission, the G7S delivers powerful torque, refined drivability and improved fuel efficiency for long-distance freight operations.

Designed with a reinforced chassis structure, advanced safety systems and air suspension stability, the G7S is built to maximise uptime while maintaining driver comfort and operational cost control.

Overview

The G7S 6x4 Prime Mover is designed for modern linehaul fleets requiring fuel efficiency, durability and high torque output. With a powerful 12.42L engine and premium European transmission technology, it provides the performance and reliability needed for long-distance freight operations across national transport corridors.

Applications

  • Interstate Linehaul Transport — Optimised for long-distance freight across NSW, VIC, QLD and national corridors.
  • B-Double & Heavy Combination Operations — Designed to handle demanding GCM requirements and high-load applications.
  • Fleet & Logistics Operators — Built for high-utilisation commercial fleets requiring reliability and efficiency.
  • General Freight & Container Transport — Suitable for curtain-siders, skeletal trailers and refrigerated combinations.

Key Highlights

High-Performance 12.42L Engine

  • Engine Model: MC13.54-61
  • Displacement: 12.42L
  • Max Power: 400 kW @ 1800 rpm
  • Max Torque: 2500 Nm @ 950–1400 rpm

Delivers strong low-end torque for smooth heavy-load acceleration and grade climbing.

ZF TraXon Automated Transmission

  • Model: ZF 12TX2621TD
  • 12-speed AMT
  • Matched PTO configuration available
  • Ratio options: 3.08 / 3.36 / 3.7

Provides efficient shifting and reduced driver fatigue for long-haul operations.

Heavy-Duty 6x4 Driveline

  • GVM: 25,000 kg
  • Front Axle Design Load: 7,000 kg
  • Rear Axle Design Load: 18,000 kg
  • Differential Locks: Interwheel & Interaxle

Ensures reliable traction and stable load distribution.

Reinforced Frame Structure

  • Main Beam Cross Section: 270 × 85 × 8 mm
  • Frame Width: Front 942 mm / Rear 762 mm
  • Trapezoidal ladder-type chassis

Designed for durability under heavy-duty freight applications.

Suspension & Stability

  • Front: Parabolic leaf springs (3 leaves)
  • Rear: 8-airbag air suspension

Enhances ride comfort and cargo stability.

Advanced Safety Systems

  • Dual Circuit Compressed Air Brake
  • EBS + ESC + HSA
  • AEBS + LDW
  • NH4 PTO compatible with TraXon

Supports compliance with modern fleet safety standards.
