G7 Series

SITRAK G7S 6x4 Tipper Cab & Chassis

Heavy-Duty Engineering for Construction & Quarry Operations.

The SITRAK G7S 6x4 Tipper Cab & Chassis is purpose-built for demanding construction, quarry and infrastructure transport applications across Australia. Powered by a 12.42L high-output engine and matched with a ZF TraXon automated transmission, the G7S Tipper delivers strong low-end torque, controlled power delivery and dependable drivability under heavy payload conditions.

With a reinforced ladder-type frame, air suspension system and advanced braking technology, the G7S 6x4 Tipper is engineered to withstand repetitive heavy-load cycles while maintaining operational efficiency and fleet reliability.

Overview

Designed for heavy-duty tipping applications, the G7S 6x4 Tipper combines powerful engine output with reinforced structural engineering. Its 6x4 configuration provides reliable traction and load balance, making it suitable for quarry haulage, civil construction and large-scale infrastructure work.

Applications

  • Quarry & Aggregate Transport — Designed for sand, gravel, rock and bulk material hauling.
  • Civil & Infrastructure Projects — Suitable for road construction, demolition and major development works.
  • High-Utilisation Construction Fleets — Built for demanding daily operation and repetitive tipping cycles.
  • Custom Tipper Body Integration — Optimised for commercial body-builder compatibility.

Key Highlights

High-Performance 12.42L Diesel Engine

  • Engine Model: 540 HP MC13.54-61
  • Displacement: 12.42L
  • Max Power: 400 kW @ 1800 rpm
  • Max Torque: 2500 Nm @ 1000–1400 rpm

Provides strong torque output for heavy-load start-up and grade performance.

ZF TraXon Automated Transmission

  • Model: ZF 12TX2621TD
  • 12-speed AMT
  • Smooth shifting for heavy-duty site applications
  • Enhanced fuel efficiency and reduced driver fatigue

Heavy-Duty 6x4 Driveline

  • GVM: 25,000 kg
  • Front Axle Design Load: 7,000 kg
  • Rear Axle Design Load: 18,000 kg
  • Main Drive Ratio: 3.08
  • Differential Locks: Interwheel & Interaxle

Ensures reliable traction and load stability on uneven construction surfaces.

Reinforced Tipper-Ready Frame

  • Main Beam Cross Section: 300 × 80 × 8 mm
  • Trapezoidal ladder-type chassis
  • Frame Width: Front 940 mm / Rear 850 mm

Engineered for durability under high-load tipping cycles.

Suspension & Stability

  • Front: Parabolic Leaf Springs (3 leaves)
  • Rear: 8-Airbag Air Suspension

Provides enhanced balance and improved load support.

Advanced Safety Systems

  • Dual Circuit Compressed Air Brake
  • EBS + ESC + HSA
  • ACC + AEBS + LDW
  • Spring Energy Air-Cut Parking Brake

Supports modern fleet safety and compliance standards.
