Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how RUMBLE Auto collects, uses, stores and protects personal information submitted through our website, enquiry forms and related online interactions.

Effective date: 8 May 2026

1. Who We Are

This website is operated by RUMBLE Auto, a NSW SITRAK dealer based in the Sydney area. This policy applies to personal information collected through this website, enquiry forms, phone or email links, cookie consent tools and related digital services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, phone number, email address, business name, enquiry type, preferred model, service request, fleet requirement and any message you submit.

We may also collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, approximate location, pages visited, date and time of visit, referring page, downloads and website usage information.

3. Why We Collect Information

  • Respond to sales, service, parts, fleet, finance or general enquiries.
  • Provide quotes, brochures, product information and follow-up support.
  • Arrange contact from our sales, service or support team.
  • Improve our website, products, customer experience and enquiry handling.
  • Measure website traffic, engagement and performance.
  • Maintain website security, prevent misuse and comply with legal obligations.

4. Enquiry Information

Information submitted through enquiry forms is used primarily to respond to your request and provide relevant product or service information. We may contact you by phone or email about products, services, finance, parts or fleet opportunities related to your enquiry.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies, consent storage and analytics tools to improve functionality, remember preferences, measure traffic and understand how visitors use the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings, although disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

More detail is available in our Cookie Policy.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party providers for website hosting, analytics, maps, video or media hosting, form handling, email delivery, enquiry management and website security. These providers may process information on our behalf where needed to operate the website or respond to enquiries.

7. Disclosure of Personal Information

We may disclose personal information to employees, contractors, service providers or related service partners where needed to respond to your enquiry, operate the website, protect our legal rights or comply with law. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

8. Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss and unauthorised access, modification or disclosure. No method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Overseas Processing

Some service providers may store or process data outside Australia. Where this occurs, we take reasonable steps to use providers and settings appropriate for the type of information involved.

10. Access and Correction

You may request access to personal information we hold about you or ask us to correct information that is inaccurate, incomplete or out of date. To make a request, contact us using the details on this page.

11. Marketing Communications

If we contact you in relation to your enquiry, product interest or business discussion, we may send relevant follow-up information. You can ask us to stop sending non-essential marketing or promotional communications by contacting us directly.

12. Links to Other Websites

Our website may link to third-party websites, including maps, social platforms, video platforms, PDF brochures or external partner pages. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

13. Children

Our website is intended for business, fleet, transport, service and commercial enquiries. It is not directed to children and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business, website features, legal requirements or privacy practices. The updated version will be published on this page.
