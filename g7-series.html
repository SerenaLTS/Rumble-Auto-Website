Series Overview

G7 adds high-output power for freight, quarry and infrastructure work.

The G7 Series is the stronger work range for fleet operators comparing prime mover and tipper choices. It is suited to transport businesses that need highway productivity, construction-site traction and clear aftersales pathways from RUMBLE Auto.

Best matched to

  • Prime mover work and regional freight movement.
  • Quarry, aggregate and heavy material transport.
  • High-utilisation fleets with repetitive load cycles.
  • Operators comparing G7 power with G5 flexibility.
