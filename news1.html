RUMBLE AUTO NEWS

RUMBLE Auto supports SITRAK truck customers across Sydney NSW.

Dealer support, model guidance and commercial vehicle pathways from Arndell Park for operators considering SITRAK trucks.

Category Dealer Update Location Arndell Park, NSW Published May 2026

RUMBLE Auto is a NSW SITRAK dealer based in the Sydney area, supporting commercial operators with truck sales enquiries, service support, genuine parts pathways and fleet planning.

SITRAK is represented across Australia through a national dealer network. From Arndell Park, RUMBLE Auto works with Sydney and NSW customers who are reviewing truck specifications, body applications, delivery requirements and ongoing service needs.

Support for model selection and fleet planning

The RUMBLE Auto team can assist operators comparing SITRAK models across key requirements such as payload, routes, axle setup, body type, annual kilometres and delivery timing.

Available model pathways include G5 Series, G7 Series and C9H trucks, with configurations suited to transport, construction, quarry, concrete and fleet applications.

Service, parts and technical support

Beyond the initial vehicle enquiry, RUMBLE Auto provides support for scheduled maintenance, diagnostics, repairs, genuine parts requests and operator guidance. This helps customers plan for the full working life of their trucks.
