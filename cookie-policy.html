Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how RUMBLE Auto uses cookies, local storage and analytics tools on our website.

Effective date: 8 May 2026

1. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files or browser storage records placed on your device when you visit a website. They can help websites remember preferences, measure performance, improve security and understand how visitors use a site.

2. How We Use Cookies

RUMBLE Auto uses cookies and similar technologies to operate the website, remember your cookie consent choice, understand website traffic and improve the experience for people researching SITRAK trucks, service support and fleet pathways.

3. Essential Website Storage

Some storage is used so the website can work properly. For example, our cookie notice records whether you accepted or dismissed the notice so it does not keep appearing on every page. This value is stored in your browser under the key rumble_cookie_consent_v2.

4. Analytics Cookies

If you accept cookies, our website may load Google Analytics to help us understand page visits, traffic patterns, device types, downloads and general engagement. Analytics helps us improve website content and customer pathways.

Our analytics configuration anonymises IP addresses where supported and is intended for aggregated website performance reporting rather than identifying individual visitors.

5. Third-Party Content

Pages on this website may include third-party services such as Google Maps, externally hosted images, PDF brochure links, social media links or analytics services. Those third parties may use their own cookies or similar technologies under their own policies.

6. Managing Cookies

You can manage or block cookies in your browser settings. You can also delete existing cookies or site data for this website. Blocking cookies may affect features such as consent preferences, embedded maps, analytics measurement or other website functionality.

7. Consent Choices

When the cookie banner appears, selecting Accept allows analytics to load. Closing or dismissing the banner records that choice and prevents analytics from loading unless you later clear site data and accept cookies on a future visit.

8. Personal Information

Some cookie or analytics data may be treated as personal information depending on context. For more detail about how RUMBLE Auto handles personal information, please read our Privacy Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when our website features, analytics tools or consent practices change. The updated version will be published on this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions about cookies or website privacy, contact RUMBLE Auto at info@rumbleauto.com.au or call (02) 9621 1886.
