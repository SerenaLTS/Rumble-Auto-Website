SITRAK G5S 8x4 Cargo Cab & Chassis

Maximum Payload Capability for Heavy Commercial Applications.

The SITRAK G5S 8x4 Cargo Cab & Chassis is engineered for high-payload commercial operations requiring enhanced axle capacity, structural strength and operational stability. Designed for construction logistics, bulk freight and heavy-duty body applications, the G5S 8x4 delivers reliable torque performance, reinforced frame durability and balanced weight distribution across four axles.

Built to meet the demands of Australian transport and infrastructure projects, this configuration provides higher load capacity while maintaining drivability and safety.

Overview

The G5S 8x4 is designed for maximum payload efficiency in heavy-duty commercial applications. With a multi-axle configuration and reinforced structural engineering, it supports higher gross vehicle mass requirements while maintaining operational stability and controlled drivability. Ideal for infrastructure, construction and bulk logistics sectors, it provides the strength required for demanding transport tasks.

Applications

  • Heavy Construction Transport — Ideal for large tray bodies, tipper builds and infrastructure supply.
  • Bulk Material & Equipment Delivery — Suitable for steel, concrete products, building materials and heavy cargo.
  • Government & Fleet Contracts — Designed for reliable high-utilisation commercial fleets.
  • Custom Body Integration — Compatible with specialised commercial body installations.

Key Highlights

Proven 7.36L Diesel Engine

  • Engine Model: 350HP MC07H.35-61 Series
  • Displacement: 7.36L
  • Max Power: 254 kW @ 2200 rpm
  • Max Torque: 1412 Nm @ 1200–1700 rpm

Provides consistent torque output for heavy load transport.

Heavy-Duty 8x4 Driveline Configuration

  • Multi-axle stability for higher payload distribution
  • Designed for demanding commercial tasks
  • Differential locks for enhanced traction

Ensures balanced weight support and improved road control.

Reinforced Ladder-Type Frame

  • Heavy-duty trapezoidal chassis design
  • Optimised cross-member strength
  • Body-builder friendly mounting structure

Built for durability under high payload cycles.

Suspension & Stability

  • Front: Parabolic leaf springs
  • Rear: Heavy-duty suspension system (as specified)

Delivers load support while maintaining ride stability.

Advanced Braking & Safety Systems

  • Dual Circuit Compressed Air Brake
  • EBS + ESC + HSA
  • AEBS + LDW

Supporting modern fleet safety requirements.
