Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how RUMBLE Auto collects, uses, stores and protects personal information submitted through our website, enquiry forms and related online interactions.

Effective date: April 16, 2026

1. Who We Are

This website is operated by RUMBLE Auto. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, you can contact us using the details below.

Email: info@rumbleauto.com.au

Phone: (02) 9621 1886

Address: 15 Contaplas St, Arndell Park NSW 2148, Australia

2. What Information We Collect

We may collect personal and technical information in the following ways.

  • Information you provide directly, including your name, phone number, email address, business name, enquiry type, preferred model, service request, fleet requirement and message.
  • Technical information collected automatically, including IP address, browser type, device type, approximate location, pages visited, date and time of visit and website usage information.
  • Information collected through cookies, analytics tools, server logs or similar technologies used to improve website performance and user experience.

3. Why We Collect Your Information

We collect and use your information for legitimate business purposes, including to:

  • respond to sales, service, parts, fleet, finance or general enquiries
  • provide quotes, brochures, product details and follow-up support
  • assess your interest in our vehicles, services or fleet solutions
  • arrange contact from our sales, service or support team
  • improve our website, products and customer experience
  • measure website traffic, engagement and performance
  • maintain website security and prevent misuse
  • comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. How We Use Enquiry Information

Information submitted through our enquiry forms is used primarily to respond to your request, provide relevant product or service information and support ongoing communication related to your enquiry.

  • contact you by phone or email
  • answer your questions or provide requested information
  • follow up regarding products, services, finance, parts or fleet opportunities
  • maintain an internal record of customer and business enquiries
  • improve how we respond to future enquiries

5. Cookies And Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, remember preferences, measure traffic and understand how visitors use the site. You can usually control cookies through your browser settings, although disabling them may affect some website functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party providers to support website operation and communication, such as website hosting, analytics, video hosting, mapping services, form handling, email delivery or enquiry management tools. These providers may process information on our behalf where necessary to operate the website or respond to enquiries.

7. Disclosure Of Personal Information

We may disclose your personal information:

  • to employees, contractors or service providers who help us operate the business
  • to related service partners where needed to respond to your enquiry
  • where required or authorised by law
  • in connection with a business restructure, transfer or sale of assets
  • where necessary to protect our legal rights, property or safety

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

8. Storage And Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss and unauthorised access, modification or disclosure. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Overseas Disclosure

Some service providers we use may store or process data outside Australia. Where this occurs, we take reasonable steps to ensure the arrangement is appropriate for the type of information involved.

10. Access And Correction

You may request access to personal information we hold about you and ask us to correct information that is inaccurate, incomplete or out of date. To make a request, please contact us using the details in this policy.

11. Marketing Communications

If we contact you in relation to your enquiry, product interest or business discussion, we may send relevant follow-up information. If you no longer wish to receive non-essential marketing or promotional communications, you can ask us to stop by contacting us directly.

12. Links To Other Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, including maps, video platforms or external partner pages. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

13. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for business, fleet, transport, service and commercial enquiries. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website.

14. Changes To This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business, website features, legal requirements or privacy practices. The updated version will be published on this page with a revised effective date.

15. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns or complaints about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us.
