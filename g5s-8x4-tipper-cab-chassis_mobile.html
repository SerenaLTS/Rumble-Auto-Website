SITRAK G5S 8x4 Tipper Cab & Chassis

Heavy-Duty Strength for Demanding Construction Applications.

The SITRAK G5S 8x4 Tipper Cab & Chassis is engineered for high-capacity construction, quarry and infrastructure transport operations across Australia. Featuring a reinforced 8x4 axle configuration, durable ladder-type chassis and Allison automatic transmission, the G5S 8x4 Tipper delivers strong torque performance, improved traction and reliable load stability under demanding site conditions.

Purpose-built for tipper body integration, this configuration provides the structural strength and operational durability required for heavy material hauling.

Overview

The G5S 8x4 Tipper is designed for high-capacity material transport in construction and quarry operations. With a reinforced driveline configuration, trapezoidal ladder-type chassis and automatic transmission control, it delivers dependable torque performance and structural durability.

Engineered to meet Australian heavy-duty fleet requirements, it provides load stability, traction performance and operational efficiency for repetitive tipping cycles.

Applications

  • Quarry & Aggregate Transport — Ideal for sand, gravel and bulk construction materials.
  • Infrastructure & Civil Projects — Designed for road building, demolition and major developments.
  • High-Capacity Construction Fleets — Built for repetitive heavy-load cycles.
  • Custom Tipper Body Installations — Optimised for commercial body-builder integration.

Key Highlights

7.36L High-Torque Diesel Engine

  • Engine Model: 350HP MC07H.35-61 Series
  • Displacement: 7.36L
  • Max Power: 254 kW @ 2200 rpm
  • Max Torque: 1412 Nm @ 1200–1600 rpm

Provides stable torque delivery under full payload conditions.

Allison Automatic Transmission

  • Model: Allison 4430P
  • Smooth automatic shifting
  • Improved site manoeuvrability
  • Reduced driver fatigue

Designed for consistent performance in stop-start construction operations.

Heavy-Duty 8x4 Driveline

  • GVM: 31,000 kg
  • Front Axle Design Load: 11,500 kg (total)
  • Rear Axle Design Load: 18,000 kg (total)
  • Main Drive Ratio: 5.29
  • Differential Locks: Interwheel & Interaxle

Ensures strong traction and stable weight distribution.

Reinforced Tipper-Ready Frame

  • Trapezoidal ladder-type chassis
  • Main Beam Cross Section: 280 × 80 × 8 mm
  • Frame Width: Front 940 mm / Rear 850 mm

Engineered for durability under heavy tipping cycles.

Suspension & Stability

  • Front: Parabolic Leaf Springs
  • Rear: Hendrickson Comfort Air Suspension

Provides improved load balance and ride stability.

Advanced Braking & Safety

  • Dual Circuit Compressed Air Brake
  • EBS + ESC + HSA
  • AEBS + LDW
  • Spring Energy Air-Cut Parking Brake

Supports modern safety compliance and fleet reliability.
